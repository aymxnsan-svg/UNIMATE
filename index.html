About Unimate

Connecting students, empowering campus commerce.

🎯 Our Mission

Unimate is a student-driven marketplace built for the campus community of Perlis. We believe every student has a skill or service to offer — and every student has a need. Unimate bridges that gap.

🌟 What We Offer

🍔 Food and Delivery

Homemade meals and snacks delivered right to your block or hostel room.

🚗 Transport and Rides

Affordable rides to and from campus, town, airports and more.

🖨️ Printing Services

Fast, affordable color and B&W printing, binding, and laminating.

📚 Tutoring

Academic support from seniors in math, science, language and more.

🎨 Design and Creative

Logos, posters, presentations and digital design for any occasion.

🏫 Why Unimate?

  • 100% student-run — by students, for students
  • Free to join and free to browse
  • Built-in complaint system for safe transactions
  • Open to all — students, staff, and the public

Terms of Service

Last updated: January 2025. Please read carefully before using Unimate.

📌 1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using Unimate, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service.

👤 2. User Accounts

  • You must provide accurate information including your real name and student/IC number.
  • You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account.
  • Unimate reserves the right to suspend accounts that violate these terms.

📦 3. Listings and Services

  • All listings must be genuine, legal, and accurately described.
  • Admin reserves the right to remove listings that violate community standards.
  • Sellers are responsible for delivering services as described.

⚠️ 4. Prohibited Activities

  • Posting false or fraudulent listings
  • Harassment or inappropriate communication
  • Selling illegal goods or services

📬 5. Complaints and Disputes

Admin will review all complaints and respond within 3 working days. Unimate acts as a mediator only.

🔒 6. Privacy

Your personal information is collected for account verification only and will not be shared with third parties.
